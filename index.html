Our bookshop resides in the beautiful Western Massachusetts town of Southampton, which lies within the Pioneer Valley with a view of Pomeroy Mountain to the West and Mt. Tom to the Northeast. It's also situated between the artistic, vibrant communities of Easthampton and Northampton and the city of Westfield. We're located in a fabulous brick building on the primary thoroughfare through town, also known as Route 10 or College Highway. It was originally constructed in 1904 as the town library, with exterior white columns and interior oak columns and other interesting architectural features. We have rare books, collections, children's books, non-fiction/fiction, ephemera, antiques, collectibles and a database with over 40,000 books online for purchase.
