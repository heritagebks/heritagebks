Does Heritage Books buy books?

Can I donate books to Heritage Books?

Yes. To maintain circulating our inventory, we ask that guidelines are followed. Please see below.

TYPES OF BOOKS AND ITEMS WE ACCEPT

  •  Very good condition
  • Spine is attached 
  • Little to no bumping to edges
  • No smells â€“ must, mold, smoke, etc.
  • No markings by notes or highlighting
  • No water damage (indicated by rippled pages or staining)
  • Minor cosmetic defects (marks, wears, cuts, bends, crushes) on the cover, spine, pages, or dustjacket
  • No textbooks, dictionaries, thesauruses, encyclopedias, price guides, and any similar subject matter

** All books are subject to approval by Heritage Books. **
